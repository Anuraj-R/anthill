The aim of each stage is to kill all the enemies on the map, while atleast one of your own units is standing. Your units has blue health bars over their head while the enemies have red health bars.



You move around the map by tapping on an empty tile. Every unit has a certain number of move points, which determines the distance it can move. The tiles that can be moved into are highlighted with a green tint.

You attack an enemy by tapping on the enemy when it is the turn on one of your units. Melee units can attack enemy units on tiles adjacent to itself. Ranged units can attack enemy units within their range. The range of the selected unit is highlighted with a yellowish tint.



The cards on the top show the order in which units move. Taping on a card shows the details of the unit. The order of activation is based on the initiative value of the unit.



There are 5 kinds of terrains in the game. Impassable, Plains, Forest, Hill and Mountain. Different terrains provide different bonus/penalty to the units standing on them. Terrain can be identified by the background color of the tile.

Forest cover. +60% ranged defence. 50% melee vulnerability.
Mountain. +2 range to ranged units.
High hills. +1 range to ranged units.
Impassable


Every kill has a chance to drop potions into your inventory. You can use potions to buff up the selected unit.

 Health Potion. Heals 50 hit points.
26

31
 Bark Skin Potion. +30 defence and ranged defence for 3 turns.


More stages get unlocked as you finish each stage. Happy hunting!
